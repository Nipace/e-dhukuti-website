About Edhukuti

e-Dhukuti is a digital platform of Rotating Saving and Credit Assocations(RoSCA). In which self-selected members of a group regularly contribute an equal amount of savings toward a common fund also called as a pot. The collected fund is distributed to an individual member who is queued to get the contribution, and the disbruishment rotates among members until every member has recieved the pooled fund.

Person-to-Person Saving & Lending Money in Disciplined Manner 

Saving in a group more than tripples your chances of saving succesfully.

  • Build tables and lists in a click
